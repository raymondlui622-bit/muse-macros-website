Got Questions?

Everything you
need to know.

Where does the 30g of protein come from? +

We use a blend of whey isolate and plant-based pea protein — depending on the flavor — to hit our protein targets without altering the taste. No mysterious "protein blends" with hidden ingredients.

How fresh are the smoothies? +

All bottles are cold-blended within 48 hours of your order and stay fresh for 5 days refrigerated. We don't make batches in advance — your order triggers the blend.

Are any flavors dairy-free or vegan? +

Yes — Dragon Fruit, Calamansi, and Passionfruit are fully plant-based and dairy-free. All other flavors use whey isolate from grass-fed cows. Each product page lists full dietary details.

Can I subscribe for weekly deliveries? +

Subscriptions are coming soon. Right now we offer one-time orders with free local delivery in the GTA. Sign up to our newsletter to be first in line when subscriptions launch.

Do you use artificial sweeteners? +

Never. Sweetness comes from whole fruits and a small amount of raw honey or maple depending on the flavor. No stevia, sucralose, aspartame, or any artificial sweetener.

How do I order? +

Click "Shop Now," choose your flavors and quantity, and checkout. We deliver refrigerated in insulated packaging. Same-day delivery available in select Toronto areas.

Still have questions?

Our team is available 7 days a week. We respond to every message — usually within a few hours.
